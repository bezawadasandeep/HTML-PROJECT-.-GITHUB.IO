Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see our email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and select cryptocurrency options. All transactions are securely processed.

How can I request a refund?

Refund requests must be made within 14 days of purchase. Please contact our support team with your order number and reason for the refund. Digital purchases may be subject to different refund policies.

Why is my game running slowly?

Performance issues can be caused by several factors. Try updating your graphics drivers, closing other applications, and checking your internet connection. For persistent issues, contact our technical support.

How do I report a player for misconduct?

You can report players directly through the game interface or by contacting our support team with details of the incident. Please include screenshots or video evidence if possible.
